Dog Breeds

Choosing a purebred is a great way to know what a dog's looks and personality might be like.
Even within breeds, there's enormous variety in the way a dog acts and reacts to the world around them. Those differences can be due to how much socialization and handling they received as a youngster, how well the owner trained them after taking them home, and -- of course -- the genetic luck of the draw. In the end, your dog's preferences and personality are as individual as you are -- and if you can accept that, you're bound to enjoy each other's companionship for life.

Advantages of Adopting a Dog:

1. The amount of self-satisfaction that you will get by adopting a dog would far surpass that when you purchase one because the amount of unconditional love you will receive from a homeless or rescued dog will be the same as that you would receive from a dog that you purchase.

2. Our very own Indian strays, apart from being absolute beauties, the rough life on streets has made them immune and healthy in numerous ways. They are naturally much more strong and tolerant to adverse conditions.

3. Adoption is free of cost! Hence, you save the costs of purchasing a dog.

4. For those who want an excellent variety of dogs to choose from, there is nothing better than a shelter or a rescue center.

5. For those who are a little older and want a slightly sober dog as a companion, a shelter or a rescue center may be a good choice as you can pick up a well-trained old dog too.

6. For every one dog that you adopt from a rescue center or shelter, one more dog is taken in its place. So in a way you actually save two lives!
